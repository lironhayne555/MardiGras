Every year in the month of February, thousands of people fill the streets of the city of New Orleans and other places in the world.

All the people in colorful costumes according to the best local tradition.

The origin of the carnival and the big parades is from 1857. A period of high French society balls in New Orleans.

Maradi Gras means: Fat Tuesday. The day when the pleasure is brought to an extreme before the Easter fast.

Everyone is invited to the biggest party in the world - Mardi Gras, the legendary carnival.
